Eligibility & Entry Requirements

Admissions are granted subject to academic suitability and seat availability.

Entry is available from Playhouse through Class IX. Applicants seeking admission into higher classes are required to submit prior academic records and may undergo an assessment to determine appropriate placement.

Admission Process

A transparent and structured four-stage procedure.

01. Application Submission

Collect and submit the admission form during designated office hours.

02. Document Verification

Submission and verification of required documents for eligibility.

03. Academic Evaluation

Assessment may be conducted for higher classes to ensure readiness.

04. Confirmation & Enrolment

Admission confirmed upon review and completion of formalities.
